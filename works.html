About Us

Lamsotic is a Talent Management and Influencer Marketing Agency founded by two cousins with a shared vision: to help content creators grow, thrive, and build sustainable careers. We work closely with influencers, providing support, guidance, and opportunities to connect with brands that align with their values and audience. We believe that success in the digital world is built on authenticity, creativity, and strong relationships. That’s why we focus on fostering meaningful collaborations between influencers and brands, ensuring that every partnership feels natural and delivers real value. At Lamsotic, we are committed to continuous learning, adapting to industry trends, and creating opportunities that help influencers turn their passion into long-term success.

Our Services

We're all about communication and transparency, making it our priority that your message is clear to everyone.

Working with relevant influencers worldwide to deliver optimal results and real value to brands while meeting their goals and key performance indicators.

Using our expertise we develop and implement specialised social media strategies to grow and develop creators and brands.
